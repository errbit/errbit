2. Update `faraday` gem from 2.14.1 to 2.14.2. [CVE-2026-33637](https://github.com/advisories/GHSA-5rv5-xj5j-3484)
3. Update `jwt` gem from 3.1.2 to 3.2.0. [CVE-2026-44351](https://github.com/advisories/GHSA-c32j-vqhx-rx3x)
